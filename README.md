# Shield Insurance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was developed as part of the CodeBasics Virtual Internship, focusing on analyzing insurance data to extract meaningful business insights.

The goal was to transform raw data into an interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders understand customer behavior, revenue trends, and policy performance.

## 🚀 [Live Dashboard](https://app.powerbi.com/reportEmbed?reportId=fac9dbd8-e9ce-455d-8f09-1e9bd20f9a1e&autoAuth=true&ctid=6b807360-f0db-4585-b934-8b35e02bb669)

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Shield Insurance wanted a data-driven solution to:
- Track revenue and customer growth
- Analyze performance across cities and age groups
- Understand sales channel effectiveness
- Improve decision-making using insights

## 📊 Dashboard Features
The dashboard consists of 3 main views:

### 1. Overview Analysis
- Total Customers & Revenue KPIs
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Customer Segmentation by City & Age Group

### 2. Sales Analysis
- Sales Mode Performance (Offline vs Online)
- Revenue & Customer Distribution by Channel
- Monthly Sales Trends

### 3. Age Group Analysis
- Policy Performance by Age Group
- Revenue Contribution by Age Segment
- Expected Settlement Analysis

## 🔍 Key Insights
- March recorded the highest revenue and customer growth
- Offline-Agent channel is the dominant sales contributor
- Delhi NCR generates the highest revenue among cities
- 31–40 age group is the most valuable customer segment
- 50+ age group shows higher claim/settlement amounts
